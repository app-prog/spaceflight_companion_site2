Interactive tools

3D Orbit Tool

Visualizes two-body orbital motion using classical orbital elements (a, e, i, Ω, ω, ν₀) with real-time interaction. Distances are normalized by Earth radius.

  • Interactive orbit geometry and satellite motion
  • Designed for teaching and conceptual understanding
  • Runs directly in a modern browser
Path: /tools/orbital-3d/dist/
Open Direct index

3D Ground Track Tool

Extends the 3D orbit visualization by adding an Earth surface model and a ground-track plot, illustrating the subsatellite point evolution under Earth rotation.

  • Earth surface rendering (procedural textures)
  • Ground track and subsatellite point visualization
  • Helpful for relating orbital inclination to latitude coverage
Path: /tools/orbital-3d2d-gtrack/dist/
